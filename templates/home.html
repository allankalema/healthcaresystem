About Our System

Our Maternal Healthcare system is designed to digitize and streamline antenatal care for pregnant mothers. With this system, mothers can easily track their appointments, receive updates, and stay informed about their health progress. Doctors and healthcare providers can efficiently monitor their patients, ensuring timely and effective care.

We also integrate GPS technology to help mothers locate nearby healthcare centers for urgent care. In the future, we aim to include vaccination tracking and other advanced features to further enhance maternal and child health.

Key Features

Appointment Tracking

Mothers can easily track their antenatal appointments and receive reminders.

GPS Integration

Locate nearby healthcare centers for urgent care using GPS technology.

Doctor-Patient Communication

Doctors can monitor and communicate with their patients effectively.
